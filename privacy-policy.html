Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to The Gula Corporation SL ("Gula", "we", "us", or "our"). We operate the Gula Club loyalty program, GULA DATA analytics platform, and related services, including integrations with third-party platforms such as Uber Eats.

Contact: marketing@thegulacorp.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Name, email address, and phone number when you register for Gula Club
  • Order history, items purchased, order amounts, and delivery addresses via Uber Eats
  • Payment information (processed securely by Uber Eats — we do not store card details)
  • Device and usage data when you interact with our platforms
  • Location data associated with your orders

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To operate and improve the Gula Club loyalty program
  • To process and track orders placed through Uber Eats
  • To analyze sales data and restaurant performance (internal analytics)
  • To send you promotional offers, rewards, and program updates (with your consent)
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Uber Eats Integration

Our GULA DATA platform integrates with the Uber Eats Marketplace API to automatically receive order and sales data from our restaurant operations. This integration allows us to:

  • Receive real-time order notifications via webhooks
  • Store order details, customer information, and payment summaries in our secure database
  • Generate analytics reports for internal business use

Data received through this integration is stored securely in Supabase and is only accessible to authorized Gula Corporation staff.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Uber Eats — as part of our restaurant partnership and API integration
  • Supabase — our secure cloud database provider
  • Legal authorities — when required by law

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Order data is retained for a minimum of 5 years for accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at marketing@thegulacorp.com.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in encrypted databases.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
